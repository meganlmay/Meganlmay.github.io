Hello and welcome to Uncork Sunshine!

This blog is dedicated to all things wine.

My name is Megan and I have been a lover of grapes ever since my first trip to Napa Valley in 2009. I always have a trip planned and cross a new city off my list every year. I have a passion for wine, specifically red wine, but I don’t discriminate. I’ve always been known for taking notes on my trips and share them with friends and friends of friends and wanted a place to further share my experiences. I was born in Westlake Village, California and grew up in Orange County. I attend the University of Wisconsin-Madison where I studied Real Estate and Finance. I have been working in commercial real estate ever since, making my way from Newport Beach to Scottsdale and currently Chicago. I work full-time and I’m getting my MBA from Northwestern’s Kellogg School of Management in the evenings. I don’t quite have free time, but one of my hobbies includes wine. I believe wine tasting and sharing a bottle of wine with friends are hobbies. I have a passion for the taste and culture of wine; there’s something about the air inside caves and classiness of five wine glasses sitting in front of you as someone shares their unique story of how their wines came to be. So please join me on my journey of learning what wine is all about, how to become a knowledgable “drinker”, where to go, what to taste, and so much more. I still have a lot to learn about wine and all its complexities, but come along on my lifelong journey of learning about wine.

Cheers,

Megan
